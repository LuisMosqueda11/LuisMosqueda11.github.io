History

In 1993, a group of adventurous friends decided to combine their passion for nature and rafting. After an exhilarating trip down the river, they realized they wanted to share that experience with others. They founded Lorem Ipsum Rafting Center, a center dedicated to offering safe and exciting excursions with a focus on fun and sustainability, they began guiding groups through stunning landscapes and challenging rapids. Since then, they have created thousands of unforgettable memories, uniting people of all ages in the spirit of adventure.
